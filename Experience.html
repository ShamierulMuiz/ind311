MEMORIAL EXPERIENCE IN MY LIFE

In 2018, my friends and I agreed to go on holiday to Pulau Perhentian after the end of our schooling. A variety of precious memories and experiences I gained while being there.

Before facing the SPM test, my friends and I were taken by a school group to participate in the SPM training program under the auspices of ECER. An unforgettable memory here is that I got to know many new people.
